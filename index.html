About University of Johannesburg

Founded on the principles of excellence, innovation, and inclusivity, the University of Johannesburg stands as a beacon of higher education in Africa. Our community engagement platform represents our commitment to bridging the gap between academic excellence and real-world impact.

Our Story

Since our establishment, we have been at the forefront of educational innovation, consistently ranking among the top universities globally. Our community engagement platform is the latest evolution in our mission to democratize quality education and create meaningful connections between learners and educators.

With over 50,000 students and world-renowned faculty, we continue to push boundaries and redefine what it means to be a modern, connected university in the digital age.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that empowers individuals to become innovative leaders, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters creativity, collaboration, and lifelong learning through cutting-edge technology and personalized educational experiences.

Our Vision

To be the leading university in Africa and a globally recognized institution that transforms lives through innovative education, groundbreaking research, and meaningful community engagement. We envision a future where education knows no boundaries and every learner has access to opportunities that unlock their full potential.

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide everything we do and shape the character of our institution.

Innovation

Embracing creativity and pioneering new approaches to education and research that push boundaries and create positive change.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in all our endeavors, from teaching and research to student support and community service.

Integrity

Maintaining the highest ethical standards, transparency, and honesty in all our interactions and decision-making processes.

Community

Building inclusive, supportive environments where diverse perspectives are valued and everyone can thrive and contribute.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. Here's how we protect and handle your information.

Data Collection & Use

Personal Information: We collect only necessary information to provide our services, including name, email, academic details, and learning preferences.

Usage Data: We track platform usage to improve user experience and provide personalized learning recommendations.

Communication: We use your contact information to send important updates, educational content, and support messages.

Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics help us enhance platform functionality and user experience.

Data Protection

Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Access Control: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your information.

Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

Compliance: We comply with POPIA, GDPR, and other applicable data protection regulations.

Your Rights

Access
